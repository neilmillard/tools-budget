# The 7th Cure: Increase Thy Ability to Earn

In the final cure of Babylon, Arkad teaches his students the **7th Cure: "Increase thy ability to earn."** He tells them that the most valuable asset they have is their own knowledge and skills.

Arkad observes that a man who seeks to learn more of his craft shall be richly rewarded. If he is an artisan, he may seek to learn the methods and tools of those more skillful in the same line. If he labors at the law or at medicine, he may consult and exchange knowledge with others of his calling.

## The Most Valuable Asset

The journey to financial freedom is a long one, and while we often focus on the tools and the math, the most important driver of your financial engine is your **earning capacity**. 

Your ability to earn is like a well. If you maintain it and deepen it, it will provide for you and your family for decades. If you neglect it, it may dry up.

## Modern Ways to Increase Your Ability to Earn

In the modern world, this 7th Cure is more relevant than ever. Here is how you can apply Arkad's wisdom today:

1. **Continuous Learning:** Don't let your skills become stagnant. Whether it's taking a course, reading books in your field, or learning a new technology, keep growing.
2. **Master Your Tools:** Just as the Babylonian artisan sought better methods, you should master the tools available to you. Understand how to leverage software, automation, and data to be more effective in your work.
3. **Build Your Network:** Arkad suggests exchanging knowledge with others. Connecting with peers and mentors can open doors and provide insights that you cannot gain alone.
4. **Cultivate Discipline:** The ability to focus and see a task through to completion is a skill in itself. It’s what separates those who dream from those who achieve.

## Wisdom for the Long Game

Financial success isn't built in a single day or with a single decision. It's built through the accumulation of hundreds of small, smart choices over years and decades.

Our goal with this site has always been to provide **"Ancient wisdom. Modern tools."** to help you make those choices. But the tools only work if you have the resources to put into them. By increasing your ability to earn, you provide more fuel for your financial operating system.

## Your Path Forward

If you're ready to take the next step in your financial journey, start by investing in yourself. It's the only investment that is guaranteed to pay dividends for the rest of your life.
