# The Power of Dividends: Your Gold Multiplying

In Babylon, Arkad teaches his students the **3rd Cure: "Make thy gold multiply."** He compares it to a farmer's crop: the original seeds are your savings (Stage 2), but the multiplication happens when those seeds produce their own offspring.

In modern investing, one of the most powerful ways to multiply your gold is through **Dividends**.

## What are Dividends?
When you own a piece of a profitable company (Stage 4), that company may choose to share its profits with you. These payments are called dividends. This is the **3rd Cure** in its purest form—it's your money "working for you" as hard as you worked for it.

Dividends aren't just extra cash; they are a key driver of long-term wealth. When you reinvest those dividends to buy more shares, you are setting off a chain reaction of compounding that can turn a modest sum into a significant fortune.

## The Modern Multiplier
To make your gold multiply, you should:
1. **Choose a diversified portfolio:** Don't put all your eggs in one basket (4th Cure). A global index fund owns thousands of companies, many of which pay regular dividends.
2. **Reinvest:** In the early stages of your journey, always reinvest your dividends. This is like the offspring of your original gold producing their own offspring.
3. **Be Patient:** Compounding takes time. The real multiplication happens in the later years of your investment journey.

## Reaching the 6th Cure
The ultimate goal of the 3rd Cure is to reach the **6th Cure: "Insure a future income."** By the time you retire, you want your investments to produce enough dividends to cover your living expenses without you ever having to sell the original shares.

## Conclusion
Make thy gold multiply. Understand the power of dividends and compounding. Start today, reinvest your gains, and watch as your small seeds grow into a vast and profitable forest.
